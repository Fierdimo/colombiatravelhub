Cartagena de Indias is one of the most fascinating tourist destinations in Colombia and all of Latin America. Its unique combination of colonial history, crystal-clear Caribbean Sea and vibrant gastronomy makes it the perfect setting for unforgettable experiences.

## Why take a tour in Cartagena?

Taking a guided tour in Cartagena allows you to make the most of every hour of your visit. Local guides know stories, secrets and corners that an independent traveler would rarely discover.

## The most popular types of tours

### Historic Center Walking Tour
The heart of Cartagena is the Walled City, declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Walking its cobblestone streets, admiring the colorful facades and learning about the history of the Spanish conquistadors is a unique experience.

### Rosario Islands Excursion
The Corales del Rosario National Natural Park is one of the most important coral ecosystems in the Caribbean. Speed boat excursions depart daily from the Muelle de los Pegasos pier and last between 6 and 8 hours.

### Chiva Rumbera Night Tour
The chiva is Colombia's traditional bus, and in Cartagena it has become an unmissable nighttime experience. The party buses cruise through the most lively neighborhoods while passengers enjoy music, dancing and local food.
